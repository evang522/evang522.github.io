About me
Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Evan! I'm a Full Stack Developer from the U.S. with 3+ years of experience in Fullstack development of Typescript and PHP applications. I have a background in business tech support and server management, but naturally my favorite area to work is in the code! I love being 'hacky' and going deep into libraries and framework implementations to better understand what goes on 'under the hood'. But what is most important to me is the art of designing applications and implementing software which best communicates the domain for which it exists.

What is Important to Me as a Developer?

The most valuable things I have learned about software have less to do with language or implementation details and more about designing classes which reflect the way a system works in reality and which provide excellent tools to further the development process. I have learned to place a high value of principles of design, which make software scalable, readable, manageable, enjoyable to work with, and communicative of the domain for which it exists. I am currently working on documenting these principles here if you would like to take a look!
